]]> https://martinposta.github.io/minimal/portfolio/evoluce/feed/ 0 Figure Spacing https://martinposta.github.io/minimal/portfolio/figure-spacing/ https://martinposta.github.io/minimal/portfolio/figure-spacing/#respond Fri, 06 Sep 2013 21:36:19 +0000 https://martinposta.github.io/minimal/?post_type=portfolio&p=513 Read More]]> Back in 2012, I started working on a short graduation film with my friend Ondřej Slavík. We both loved puppet films (and we still do) so we decided to make our own. We didn’t know how to make one but we wanted to try anyway. We split the making process – Ondřej did all the analog part (making puppets, physical sets,…), I did the digital part (digital assets, postproduction, edit,…). Yet we didn’t plan to do just the movie! Oh boy no, that wouldn’t be enough! I also wanted to make a lot of other materials to help promote the film – like posters, postcards, webpage, flash game! Well, as you may have guessed, apart from all of the promo materials, the movie was sadly never finished.C
